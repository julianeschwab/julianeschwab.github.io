About me

My name is Juliane Schwab, I am a cognitive scientist spezialized in theoretical and psycholinguistics. I work as terminologist and software product manager at the translation and language service provider oneword GmbH (Böblingen, Germany).

I have previous experience as lab manager at the Max Planck Institute for Biological Cybernetics (Tübingen, Germany) and as post-doctoral researcher at the Department of Linguistics at the University of Tübingen.
My PhD in Cognitive Science was supervised by Prof. Dr. Mingya Liu (Humboldt University of Berlin) and Prof. Dr. Jutta L. Mueller (University of Vienna). In my dissertation, I investigated the semantics, pragmatics, and processing of polarity items using psycho- and neurolinguistic methods.

While I have left academia, but you can continue to find records (publications, talks, teaching materials) of my contributions to the field here.
